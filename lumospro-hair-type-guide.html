LumosPro Advanced Repair Hair Serum: Which Hair Types Benefit Most?

LumosPro's BioFusion-7 formula was designed with versatility in mind. Here's how it performs across different hair types:

Bleached / Color-Treated Hair ★★★★★

Best use case for LumosPro. Bleaching destroys keratin in the cortex — exactly what BioFusion-7 replaces. Use 2–3 drops on damp hair after every wash. Expect significant improvement within 2–3 weeks.

Heat-Damaged Hair ★★★★★

Repeated heat styling creates micro-fractures in the hair cortex. LumosPro fills these fractures with keratin, restoring elasticity and reducing breakage. Daily use recommended.

Fine Hair ★★★★☆

Works well with 1 drop — don't over-apply or it can look flat. Great for frizz control without weight. Not as dramatic as on coarser damaged hair.

Curly / Coily Hair ★★★★☆

Excellent for restoring moisture and reducing frizz in curly patterns. Apply to soaking-wet hair and scrunch in. Helps define curls and reduce breakage at contact points.

Coarse / Thick Hair ★★★★★

Use 3–4 drops liberally. Coarse hair benefits enormously from the cortex repair — frizz reduction and smoothing effects are most dramatic on this hair type.
