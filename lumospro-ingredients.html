LumosPro Hair Serum: Ingredient Analysis

What's actually inside LumosPro Advanced Repair Hair Serum — and does the science hold up?

BioFusion-7 Keratin Complex

The star ingredient. LumosPro's BioFusion-7 is a low-molecular-weight hydrolyzed keratin that penetrates the hair cuticle rather than simply coating it. This is the fundamental difference between LumosPro and surface-coating serums like Moroccanoil. The smaller keratin molecules fill cortical gaps left by chemical or heat damage, restoring tensile strength and elasticity.

Argan Oil + Ceramide Complex

Seals the cuticle after keratin repair, locking moisture in and environmental damage out. The ceramide complex mimics the natural lipid structure of healthy hair.

Panthenol (Pro-Vitamin B5)

Attracts and retains moisture, adding volume and improving combability. Works synergistically with the keratin complex.

Scientific Verdict

The ingredient list is well-formulated and the clinical claims are supported by the chemistry. LumosPro is one of the few hair serums where the science matches the marketing.
